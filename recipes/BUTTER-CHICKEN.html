BUTTER CHICKEN

Butter Chicken, the ultimate comfort food for family will love! Make the best flavour-packed,creamy,delicious and authentic butter chicken at home with this easy recipe. Serve it with Butter Naan, Pulav, Chapati for a proper indian meal.

INGRIDIENTS

MARINADE

    • ▢ 1/2 cup plain yoghurt , full fat
      ▢ 1 tbsp lemon juice
      ▢ 1 tsp tumeric powder
      ▢ 2 tsp garam masala
      ▢ 1/2 tsp chilli powder or cayenne pepper powder
      ▢ 1 tsp ground cumin
      ▢ 1 tbsp ginger, freshly grated
      ▢ 2 cloves garlic, crushed
      ▢ 1.5 lb / 750 g chicken thigh fillets, cut into bite size pieces

    • CURRY

    • ▢ 2 tbsp (30 g) ghee or butter, OR 1 tbsp vegetable oil
      ▢ 1 cup tomato passata (aka tomato puree)
      ▢ 1 cup heavy / thickened cream
      ▢ 1 tbsp sugar
      ▢ 1 1/4 tsp salt

    • TO SERVE (CHOOSE)

    • ▢ Basmati rice
      ▢ White rice
      ▢ Coriander/cilantro (optional)

    • INSTRUCTIONS

    • 1. Optional blitz: for an extra smooth sauce, combine the Marinade ingredients (except the chicken) in a food processor and blend until smooth.
      2. Marinade: Combine the Marinade ingredients with the chicken in a bowl.
      3. Cover and refrigerate overnight, or up to 24 hours (minimum 3 hrs).
      4. Cook chicken: Heat the ghee (butter or oil) over high heat in a large fry pan.
      5. Take the chicken out of the Marinade but do not wipe or shake off the marinade from the chicken (but don’t pour the Marinade left in the bowl into the fry pan).
      6. Place chicken in the fry pan and cook for around 3 minutes, or until the chicken is white all over (it doesn’t really brown because of the Marinade).
      7. Sauce: Add the tomato passata, cream, sugar and salt.
      8. Also add any remaining marinade left in the bowl.
      9. Turn down to low and simmer for 20 minutes.
      10.Do a taste test to see if it needs more salt.
      11.Garnish with coriander/cilantro leaves if using.
      12.The Butter Chicken is served with very easy No Yeast Flatbread and no-fry papadums.
